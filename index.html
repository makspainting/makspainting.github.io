Bio

Born in 1997.. Makrand is a young fine art artist. Having spent most of his life in noida, Makrand’s work depicts pages from his dreams. He started painting a decade ago at the age of seven moving through various styles of art and learning new techniques of paintings. In a single canvas, one can witness the ordinary and the complex, unity and solitude at the same time. Such waves of expressions has led him to create works inspired by abstract, cubism & surrealism.

Makrand has been highly influenced by his mother which reflects through his use of vibrant colour and textures, while his process has been a self learning experience beginning with concept sketches using photographic references and taking them into the canvas to give life.
